## 1. Service Overview

PhotoLynk is a photo and video backup application that operates in three distinct modes:

- **Local Mode**: Your data is stored on a server you own and operate on your local network.
- **Remote Mode**: Your data is stored on a server you own and operate, accessible over the internet.
- **StealthCloud Mode**: Your data is encrypted on your device before upload and stored on StealthLynk-operated infrastructure.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Local and Remote Modes

When using Local or Remote modes, **all data remains on servers you own and control**. StealthLynk LLC does not have access to, collect, or store:

- Your photos, videos, or media files
- Your email address or password
- Your device identifiers
- Any file metadata or content

**You are solely responsible** for the security, maintenance, and backup of your self-hosted server and all data stored thereon.

### 2.2 StealthCloud Mode

When using StealthCloud mode, we collect and process:

- **Account Information**: Email address for authentication purposes
- **Device Identifiers**: Hardware device identifiers for security and account recovery
- **Encrypted Data**: Photos and videos encrypted on your device before transmission
- **Encrypted Metadata**: File manifests and metadata, encrypted on your device
- **Subscription Information**: Payment and subscription status (processed by third-party payment processors)
- **Usage Data**: Storage quota utilization and upload/download activity logs

**Important**: All media files and metadata are encrypted on your device using industry-standard encryption (XSalsa20-Poly1305) before transmission. StealthLynk LLC stores only encrypted data and **does not possess the decryption keys**. We cannot access, view, or decrypt your files.

### 2.3 Information Collected Automatically

The Service may automatically collect:

- Device type and operating system version
- Application version
- Error logs and crash reports (anonymized)
- Network connection type

## 4. Data Encryption and Security

### 4.1 StealthCloud Encryption

StealthCloud implements end-to-end encryption:

- Files are encrypted on your device before upload using TweetNaCl secretbox (XSalsa20-Poly1305)
- Each file is split into encrypted chunks
- Encryption keys are derived from your credentials and stored only on your device
- StealthLynk LLC cannot decrypt your data

### 4.2 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

- Password hashing using bcrypt
- TLS/HTTPS encryption for data in transit
- Device-bound authentication
- Rate limiting and abuse prevention

**However**, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.
