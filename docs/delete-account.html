PhotoLynk Account Deletion Request

To permanently delete your PhotoLynk account and all associated data:

  1. Open the PhotoLynk app on your device
  2. Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account
  3. Follow the confirmation prompts
  4. Contact support if you encounter issues: support@photolynk.app

What gets deleted: Your account, all photos/videos, backup data, encryption keys, and account metadata.

Timeline: Deletion is permanent and occurs within 30 days of your request.

Important: This action cannot be undone. All your data will be permanently removed.

Contact: support@photolynk.app for any questions.
